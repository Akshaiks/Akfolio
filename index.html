MediReach ‚Äì A Smart Telemedicine Platform

MediReach is a telemedicine platform designed to provide remote healthcare access. It enables video consultations with doctors while integrating real-time vital monitoring. The system also includes prescription printing and data storage for medical history analysis. Vital Monitoring Module collects real-time patient vitals, allowing doctors to remotely track critical health parameters such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen levels. The **Doctor Interface** provides real-time monitoring during consultations, ensuring that medical professionals can make informed decisions based on live data. Additionally, the Data Management Module stores patient history for long-term health tracking and analytics, improving continuity of care. The Prescription System generates printed prescriptions post-consultation, streamlining the treatment process and ensuring patients receive accurate medical instructions.
